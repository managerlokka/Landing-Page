❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes! You get a 4-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required.
How does the subscription work? ▼
Choose a monthly plan (Starter, Pro, or Enterprise) based on your order volume. Contact us via WhatsApp to activate.
What happens after the trial ends? ▼
After 4 days, subscribe to continue. Your data is preserved — just activate a key and pick up where you left off.
How are orders counted? ▼
Usage is based on orders created during your billing cycle. Deleted/cancelled orders do not reduce the count.
Do deleted orders reduce my usage count? ▼
No. Once created, an order counts permanently. This ensures fair and consistent billing.
How do updates work? ▼
The app auto-checks for updates. When available, update with one click. All subscribers get updates included.
Can I upgrade my plan later? ▼
Absolutely! Contact us via WhatsApp to upgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately.
Is my data safe? ▼
Yes. All business data is stored locally. We only use cloud for subscription validation — your data never leaves your PC.
